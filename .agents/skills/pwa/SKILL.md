When editing PWA capability or offline caching:
1. The web app manifest is located at [manifest.json](file:///c:/laragon/www/PJBL-EUYY/public/manifest.json).
2. The service worker is located at [sw.js](file:///c:/laragon/www/PJBL-EUYY/public/sw.js).
3. The service worker caches key assets like CSS, images, and offline pages.
4. PWA registration occurs in [app.blade.php](file:///c:/laragon/www/PJBL-EUYY/resources/views/layouts/app.blade.php) header.
